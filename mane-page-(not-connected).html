What is this project about?

We created Crypto Dust (CDUST) — a mining platform that turns unused tokens in your wallet ("crypto dust") into real value.
Many crypto wallets are filled with old or worthless tokens that have lost their relevance. They can’t be sold for profit and just sit there as dead weight. Our solution: burn those unused tokens and earn CDUST in return.

Why will the token grow in value?

To mine CDUST, users must burn useless or outdated tokens on the Solana network.
The more people want to mine, the more tokens get burned — which increases the scarcity of CDUST. As demand rises, so does the value of the token, creating a sustainable growth loop driven by community activity.
