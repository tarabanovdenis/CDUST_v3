CryptoDust to Value

Everyone in the crypto world knows what "dust" is—small leftover tokens that accumulate in wallets and usually just sit there, unused. We offer an innovative way to turn your dust into mining power.

Project Idea

Your meme tokens no longer have to take up space in your balance! We’ve created a platform where you can exchange meme tokens for Hash Power to start mining our CDUST token. Instead of holding onto useless leftovers, you’ll have the opportunity to earn through mining and become part of our economy.

Abstract

CDUST is an innovative Web3 mining game built on the Solana blockchain. It combines virtual cryptocurrency mining with real-time, interactive gameplay. This document outlines the core mechanics, tokenomics, and principles of the CDUST project to create a sustainable and rewarding ecosystem.

Introduction

The expansion of blockchain and DeFi has opened the door to gamifying finance. CDUST builds on this momentum with a new virtual mining experience on Solana, offering players the opportunity to mine tokens using crypto dust instead of GPUs.

Mining Process

After connecting your wallet to the site, you immediately get access to mining. Rewards in CDUST tokens are distributed daily, based on the amount of mining power (Crypto Dust) each user has.

Mining is divided into seasons. Each week, the amount of Crypto Dust distributed to all users is recalculated. This dynamic distribution ensures a fair balance between newcomers and long-time players.

Acquiring Crypto Dust

You can easily swap any of your tokens for Crypto Dust—the key mining resource—directly from your wallet. Open the "Swap" tab and convert your unused crypto dust into Crypto Dust.

Crypto Dust is valid for 24 hours and then resets. You can purchase a new amount daily. This limited lifespan ensures economic balance and prevents legacy users from permanently dominating mining rewards.

Claiming Rewards

Your accumulated CDUST tokens can be claimed daily. Once claimed, tokens are automatically sent to your withdrawal address.

Upgrade Strategy

To increase your share of mining rewards, you can swap larger amounts of tokens for Crypto Dust. Any Solana-based token is supported, including SOL itself.
