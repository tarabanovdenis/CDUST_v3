HOW TO MINE

Registration

Visit our website and connect your crypto wallet. Once connected, you will receive a unique wallet address for mining.

Deposit SOL

Send SOL to your mining wallet. The minimum deposit is 0.105 SOL, which includes a transaction fee of 0.005 SOL.

Purchase Hash Power

0.1 SOL = 100 Hash Power.
Hash Power can be purchased once every 24 hours. Limited Hash Power stops strong players from ruling forever and gives newcomers a fair chance.

Start Mining

After purchasing Hash Power, you will automatically participate in the mining process for CDUST tokens.

Claiming Rewards

Rewards are credited every 15 minutes.
You can claim them at any time by specifying your withdrawal address.

Upgrade Strategy

Increase your Hash Power daily by depositing SOL to secure a larger share of rewards.
